# About Garrett

Garrett Finnell is a father of four who works a full-time systems admin job at Mid-America Christian University ($50k/year) while running three side businesses:

**Vows & Vinyl DJ Co.** (vowsok.com) — A Catholic-oriented mobile wedding DJ service (~$12k/year). Stage name: DJ Sanctus. Recently added photo booth service ($4.5k investment, ~$2k revenue so far). Goal is multi-op expansion with additional DJs.

**McGarry Homes** (mcgarryhomesokc.com) — Real estate agent at a small Catholic brokerage. Primarily residential, heavy on first-time homebuyers. Three years experience. Runs first-time homebuyer webinars for archdiocese engagement couples (5/year, low attendance so far). Target: 12+ sales/year.

**Cana Wedding Collective** (canaok.com) — Marketing platform/firm for banded-together Catholic wedding vendors. Charges $50/closed lead. Currently local, goal is national expansion through diocese network. This is the highest-potential business if scaled into a platform.

**The Funnel:** Cana generates leads → DJ/photo booth bookings → satisfied clients become warm real estate leads → target 2 transactions per client over 10 years. More people in the funnel = more sales across all lines.

**Family is priority one.** Garrett protects family time fiercely. Efficiency and automation matter because they buy back time.

**Catholic identity is a business differentiator**, not just a personal value. It's the thread connecting all three businesses and the reason the niche is defensible.
